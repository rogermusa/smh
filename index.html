Welcome to New Tecumseth

Situated in the beautiful Nottawasaga Valley in Simcoe County, New Tecumseth offers the perfect blend of small-town charm and modern amenities. Comprised of Alliston (the largest urban center), Beeton, and Tottenham, this vibrant community is less than an hour's drive from the Greater Toronto Area.

In 2019, New Tecumseth was recognized as one of the Top 5 Best Communities in Canada by McLean's magazine, reflecting its exceptional quality of life, strong economy, and community spirit.

Stevenson Memorial Hospital

For nearly 100 years, Stevenson Memorial Hospital has been providing compassionate, high-quality care to residents in New Tecumseth and surrounding communities. As a cornerstone of healthcare in the region, the hospital continues to grow and evolve alongside the community it serves.

The hospital features an inpatient unit, day surgery, OB/GYN unit, and a busy Emergency Department. On-site specialists include anesthetists, general surgeons, obstetricians, and hospitalists. Visiting specialists also provide outpatient services and perform surgeries.

Six family medicine clinics serve the New Tecumseth community, including a Family Health Organization with offices in Tottenham and Alliston, plus a Family Health Team.

Medical Education Opportunities

Stevenson Memorial Hospital partners with the Rural Ontario Medical Program (ROMP) to provide valuable learning experiences for medical learners in a community hospital setting. This partnership offers unique opportunities to gain hands-on experience while working alongside experienced healthcare professionals.

Learning Environment

As a medical learner at Stevenson Memorial Hospital, you'll benefit from:

  • Personal mentorship from dedicated physicians
  • Diverse patient cases in a growing community
  • Experience across multiple departments (ER, Surgery, OB/GYN)
  • Greater responsibility and hands-on learning opportunities
  • Exposure to a wide range of medical conditions
  • Work-life balance in a supportive community setting

With a thriving medical community and growing demand for healthcare professionals, New Tecumseth offers excellent opportunities for career development and community integration. The hospital is currently seeking physicians across multiple specialties, providing exciting opportunities for medical learners to establish their careers.

Hospital Redevelopment Project

Transforming Healthcare for Our Community

Stevenson Memorial Hospital is embarking on an exciting redevelopment project that will modernize and expand our facilities to meet the growing needs of our community. This transformation represents a significant investment in healthcare infrastructure and will provide an enhanced environment for both patients and healthcare professionals.

Key Features of the Redevelopment:

  • Triple the current hospital size to 147,100 square feet
  • Increase inpatient bed capacity by 20%
  • Triple the size of the Emergency Department
  • New MRI suite within the Diagnostic Imaging Department
  • Three new, state-of-the-art operating rooms
  • Modernized birthing suites and dedicated Obstetrics floor
  • Enhanced laboratory space
  • New elevated helipad for improved patient transport

Construction is expected to progress throughout 2025, with a Request for Proposals already issued to prequalified companies. This is an exciting time to join Stevenson Memorial Hospital and be part of its transformation!
